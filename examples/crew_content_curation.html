AI-Crew Use Case: Content Curation and Dissemination Crew

This crew of AI avatars works together to find, summarize, and distribute information on a specific topic across various platforms. This automates the process of staying updated and sharing relevant content with an audience.

Crew Overview

The Content Curation Crew can monitor multiple sources, intelligently process information, and then disseminate it through appropriate channels, acting as an automated news desk or content aggregator.

Avatar Role: News Scraper

Function: Monitors RSS feeds, specific websites (if parsable), or internal databases for new content related to a predefined topic. It acts as the primary information gatherer.

Avatar Configuration (`profiles/news_scraper_bot.yaml`)

name: "NewsScraperBot"
personality: "A tireless and efficient information gatherer. Focuses on identifying and extracting new content from designated sources. Provides raw, unfiltered data for further processing."
llm_model: "llama3:8b" # Or a smaller, faster model if only extracting
llm_options:
  temperature: 0.1 # Very low temperature for factual extraction
  top_p: 0.5
# Internal mechanism to read RSS/web content (not a direct connector, but an internal process)

Prompting Strategy & RAG

This bot might not use LLMs for generation, but rather for filtering or categorizing content. Its RAG could include rules for identifying relevant articles or data structures for different content types.

Avatar Role: Summarizer Bot

Function: Receives raw content from the News Scraper, uses an LLM to summarize it concisely, and extracts key highlights suitable for social media or brief announcements.

Avatar Configuration (`profiles/summarizer_bot.yaml`)

name: "SummarizerBot"
personality: "A concise and insightful summarizer. Transforms lengthy articles into digestible summaries, highlighting the most important points. Always maintains neutrality and accuracy."
llm_model: "llama3:8b" # Or a model good at summarization
llm_options:
  temperature: 0.4
  top_p: 0.8
# No direct external connector, receives input internally

Prompting Strategy & RAG

Uses a custom prompt template for summarization. RAG knowledge base could include guidelines on summary length, key phrase extraction, or specific terminology to include/exclude. Instructed to produce summaries of a certain length and focus.

Avatar Role: Social Media Publisher

Function: Takes summarized content from the Summarizer Bot and publishes it to various social media channels (e.g., Telegram, Discord, Mastodon), adapting the tone and format for each platform.

Avatar Configuration (`profiles/social_publisher_bot.yaml`)

name: "SocialPublisherBot"
personality: "A versatile and engaging social media communicator. Adapts content to suit different platforms and audiences, ensuring maximum reach and impact. Always adheres to platform guidelines."
llm_model: "llama3:8b"
llm_options:
  temperature: 0.7 # Higher temperature for more engaging copy
  top_p: 0.9
# Telegram, Discord, Mastodon credentials in .env

Prompting Strategy & RAG

Uses custom prompt templates for each social media platform. RAG knowledge base includes platform-specific character limits, hashtag best practices, and audience engagement strategies. Instructed to rephrase summaries for different platforms and add relevant hashtags/emojis.

Avatar Role: Archivist Bot

Function: Stores all original content and summaries in a searchable archive (e.g., sends to an internal email address for record-keeping, or adds to a RAG knowledge base for future reference).

Avatar Configuration (`profiles/archivist_bot.yaml`)

name: "ArchivistBot"
personality: "A meticulous and reliable record-keeper. Ensures all processed content is properly categorized and stored for future retrieval. Prioritizes data integrity and accessibility."
llm_model: "llama3:8b" # Or a smaller model
llm_options:
  temperature: 0.1
  top_p: 0.5
# Email credentials (for internal record-keeping) or direct DB access

Prompting Strategy & RAG

Might use LLMs for categorization or metadata extraction. Its RAG could include archiving policies, data retention rules, or indexing strategies. Instructed to process content for storage and ensure it's retrievable.

Collaboration within the Crew (using Internal Event Bus)

  • Information Discovery: The News Scraper identifies and extracts new content from its sources. Upon finding relevant content, it publishes an internal message (e.g., new_content_available) to the event bus, including the raw content.
  • Content Summarization: The Summarizer Bot subscribes to new_content_available messages. Upon receiving raw content, it processes it, condenses it into key highlights, and then publishes a content_summarized message, including the summary.
  • Multi-Platform Dissemination: The Social Media Publisher subscribes to content_summarized messages. For each summary, it crafts platform-specific posts for Telegram, Discord, etc., and sends them via the respective connectors.
  • Archiving and Knowledge Base Update: The Archivist Bot also subscribes to both new_content_available and content_summarized messages. It stores both the original content and the summaries, ensuring they are properly indexed and stored in the RAG knowledge base for future queries.
  • Dynamic Workflow: The internal event bus enables a decoupled and dynamic workflow. Each bot acts autonomously based on the events it subscribes to, allowing for flexible expansion and modification of the content pipeline.
