AI-Crew Use Case: Telegram/Discord Community Moderator (Q&A)

This use case involves an AI-Crew avatar participating in a community chat (Telegram or Discord). The avatar's primary role is to answer questions from members based on a shared knowledge base (RAG), provide helpful links, or direct users to appropriate resources, thereby reducing the load on human moderators.

Avatar Configuration Example (`profiles/community_helper.yaml`)

name: "CommunityHelper"
personality: "An enthusiastic and helpful community member. Always ready to assist with questions and share knowledge. Keeps responses concise and friendly."
llm_model: "llama3:8b"
llm_options:
  temperature: 0.6
  top_p: 0.9
# ... other avatar details (e.g., Telegram/Discord credentials in .env)

Prompting Strategy

The avatar's replies are generated using the telegram_message_template.txt or discord_message_template.txt, augmented with RAG context. The prompt guides the LLM to:

  • Directly answer the user's question if the information is available and relevant in the RAG knowledge base.
  • Provide relevant links, code snippets, or short explanations from the RAG context.
  • Maintain a friendly, supportive, and encouraging tone appropriate for a community setting.
  • If the question is outside the scope of the knowledge base, politely indicate that or suggest where else the user might find help.

RAG Knowledge Base Content

The RAG knowledge base (ingested into PostgreSQL with pg_vector) for this avatar would typically contain:

  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) specific to the community or project.
  • Community rules and guidelines.
  • Links to official documentation, tutorials, or external resources.
  • Information about common discussion topics, events, or announcements.
  • Details about how to get help from human moderators or administrators.

Example RAG context provided to the LLM: Relevant information from knowledge base: [FAQ: How to join server], [Link: Official documentation]
